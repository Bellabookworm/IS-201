One of the beautiful things about the temple is that one cannot be in it without thinking of Christ. Everything about it from its pointed peak to its intricate designs points to the centerpiece in God's great plan of happiness. Jesus Christ, the Son of God. He died so that we could live again, and He lived so that we could see a better way to be. Among other things, Christ exemplified the perfect way to love in the following ways.

  1. Ministering to the One
    • Comforting the woman caught in adultery
    • Healing the man stricken with palsy
    • Teaching Simon Peter about what it meant to be a true disciple
    • Healing Peter's mother-in-law
  2. Loving without restraint
    • Whether a leper, a sinner, a publican, or a pharisee, Christ was ready and waiting to receive them.
